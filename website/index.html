Make it yours

Seven themes. One click.

Tap a theme and watch Solace transform — these are real screenshots, not mockups.

…plus Sepia, Arctic, Dark and Slate — each with a Photoshop-style accent picker.

Tune every detail

Built to feel like yours.

A full theme gallery, an HSV accent colour wheel, adjustable frost, custom backgrounds and layout — all in a calm settings panel. Change one thing, or everything.

Private by design

Passwords, locked down.

Save logins to a vault encrypted by your operating system's keychain. Solace fills them in automatically, imports from any browser via CSV, and never syncs them to a server — because there is no server.

Block the noise

Quiet by default.

uBlock Origin ships built in and switched on. Pair it with Do-Not-Track, Global Privacy Control, and clear-on-exit for cookies, cache and history. You're in control of every toggle.

Off the record

Truly private windows.

A genuine incognito mode — an ephemeral session that keeps no history, cookies or site data on disk, wrapped in its own focused look so you always know when you're private.

Stay in flow

Downloads, handled.

Live progress toasts with speed, a tidy downloads panel, and a right-click menu to open, reveal, copy or remove — so grabbing files never breaks your stride.

A private AI assistant

Ask Solace AI from the new-tab page using your own key, kept on your device. Pages can never reach it.

Smart omnibox

Type a URL to go, or words to search. Switch search engines anytime in settings.

Extensions

Load unpacked Chromium extensions with content scripts and background pages.

Ready when you are

Bring some calm to your browsing.

Free, sets up in seconds, and keeps your data on your machine.
